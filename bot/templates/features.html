Sentiment Analysis

Our AI chatbot can detect the emotional tone behind text conversations, recognizing when a customer is frustrated, satisfied, or confused. This allows the chatbot to adapt its responses accordingly or escalate to a human agent when necessary.

  • Detects positive, negative, and neutral sentiment
  • Identifies emotional intensity
  • Adapts responses based on detected sentiment
  • Tracks sentiment trends over time

Contextual Understanding

Unlike basic chatbots that respond to individual messages in isolation, our AI chatbot maintains context throughout the conversation. It remembers previous exchanges and understands references to earlier topics, creating more natural, human-like interactions.

  • Maintains conversation history
  • Understands pronoun references
  • Recognizes topic continuity
  • Follows complex conversation flows

Personalization

Our chatbot learns from each interaction to provide increasingly personalized experiences. By recognizing returning users and remembering their preferences, the chatbot can offer tailored recommendations and responses that feel custom-made for each user.

  • User recognition
  • Preference tracking
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Adaptive conversation style

Smart Handover

Our AI chatbot knows when to pass a conversation to a human agent. By recognizing complex queries, high emotional distress, or specific request patterns, the chatbot ensures customers always receive the appropriate level of support.

  • Automatic escalation triggers
  • Smooth transition to human agents
  • Complete conversation history transfer
  • Priority-based queuing
