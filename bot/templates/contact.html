Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to implement the chatbot?

Implementation time varies depending on your specific requirements and the complexity of your business. Basic implementations can be completed in as little as 2-3 weeks, while more complex integrations may take 4-8 weeks.

Can I customize the chatbot to match my brand?

Yes, our AI chatbot is fully customizable. You can adapt the appearance, language, and personality to match your brand identity and communication style.

What languages does the chatbot support?

Our chatbot supports over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and more. If you need a specific language, please contact our sales team.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with all features included. You can sign up for a trial account on our website with no credit card required.
